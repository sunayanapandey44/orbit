Events

Pre Events

At the Orbit Engineering Expo, we offer a variety of pre-events designed to inspire, enhance, and engage participants. These events are crafted to challenge your creativity and push the boundaries of innovation, providing valuable opportunities for learning and growth before the main event.

CAD Workshop

A preparatory session for participants to enhance their skills in computer-aided design, learning advanced techniques.

Surveying Workshop

A hands-on workshop covering essential surveying tools and techniques, preparing participants for the Survey Challenge.

Paper Bridge and Popsicle Workshop

A creative session guiding participants on bridge construction techniques for the paper and popsicle bridge competitions.

Proposal Writing Workshop

A workshop designed to teach participants how to write clear and effective project proposals, essential for engineering and architecture projects.

Ideation Bootcamp

An interactive session aimed at helping participants brainstorm and develop innovative ideas, preparing them for pitching and implementation in tech-driven projects.

Git and Github Workshop

A practical workshop on version control using Git and GitHub, where participants will learn how to collaborate on projects, manage code efficiently, and contribute to open-source development.
